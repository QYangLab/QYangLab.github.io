### 🔬 Research Directions

LoT Lab focuses on three key research themes to understand the fundamental components of intelligence:

- 🧠 **Intuitive Physics as World Model**  
  Exploring how the brain builds internal models of the physical world to enable perception, prediction, and simulation of the environment.

- 🎯 **Bayesian Theory of Mind**  
  Investigating how the brain models the minds of others (ToM), enabling social inference and dynamic strategy updates during interaction.

- 💭 **Probabilistic Language of Thought in Games**  
  Studying how the brain develops a compositional and generalizable probabilistic language of thought during complex gameplay—supporting knowledge construction and transfer.

We work closely with leading experimental neuroscience labs and adopt a cross-species (human, monkey, rodent) and cross-modal (behavioral experiments, neural recordings, mathematical modeling) approach to build a unified cognitive framework.  

Our goal is to systematically reveal the computational principles underlying **perception, decision-making, and symbolic reasoning** in the brain.

### 🌱 Lab Philosophy

We believe that great science stems from genuine passion and persistent curiosity. LoT Lab embraces the following research culture:

1. **Balance breadth and depth** – Pursue broad knowledge while cultivating deep expertise in your own research direction.  
2. **Bridge theory and data** – Maintain creative tension between data-driven and hypothesis-driven approaches, aiming ultimately for theory-driven science.  
3. **Be a bridge across disciplines** – Strive to produce interdisciplinary work respected by experts in each contributing field.
